Hamburger

Ingredients for making a hamburger:

  • Ground beef
  • Black pepper
  • Hamburger buns
  • Tomatoes
  • Onions

pizza

The basic ingredients of pizza are:

  • Pizza dough (made from flour, yeast, water, salt, and sometimes olive oil)
  • Tomato sauce (made from crushed tomatoes and various spices and seasonings)
  • Cheese (usually mozzarella, but other types )

shaorma

The basic ingredients of shawarma are:

  • Thinly sliced meat (usually chicken, but can also be beef, or a mixture of meats)
  • Pita bread or wrap
  • Vegetables (such as tomatoes, and cucumbers)

Sausages

The basic ingredients of sausages are:

  • Ground meat (usually beef, pork, or chicken)
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Bread

Hamburger

Instructions:

1-Mix together the ground beef, salt, pepper, onion powder, and garlic powder. 2-Shape the mixture into patties.3- the patties on a skillet or grill until fully cooked. 4-Place a slice of cheese on each patty and let it melt. 5-Assemble the hamburger by placing the patty on a bun,adding vegetables and hamburger sauce. 6-Serve hot with French fries or other side dishes.

pizza

Instructions:

1- your oven to 425°F (220°C). 2-Roll out the pizza dough into a round shape. 3-Spread tomato sauce over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges. 4-Sprinkle cheese over the tomato sauce. 5-Add your desired toppings. 6-Bake the pizza in the preheated oven for 10-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is melted and bubbly. 7-Remove the pizza from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes. 8-Slice the pizza and serve hot.

Shaorma

Instructions:

1-Marinate the thinly sliced meat in the shawarma seasoning blend for at least 30 minutes. 2-Heat a skillet or grill over medium-high heat. 3-Cook the marinated meat until fully cooked and browned. 4-Warm up the pita bread or wrap. 5-Place the cooked meat on the pita bread or wrap. 6-Add the vegetables to the wrap. 7-Drizzle the sauce over the vegetables. 8-Roll up the pita bread or wrap tightly. 9-Serve hot.

Sausages

Instructions:

1- a mixing bowl, combine the ground meat, salt, black pepper, garlic powder, and paprika. 2-Mix well until the ingredients are evenly distributed. 3-If using sausage casings, stuff the meat mixture into the casings and twist to form individual sausages. 4-If not using casings, shape the meat mixture into sausage patties. 5-Heat a skillet or grill over medium-high heat. 6-Cook the sausages until fully cooked and browned on all sides. 7-Serve hot with your desired side dishes and condiments.

Pasta

Instructions:

1-Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. 2-Add the pasta to the boiling water and cook according to the package instructions, stirring occasionally. 3-Drain the pasta in a colander and rinse with cold water to stop the cooking process. 4-Drizzle a little olive oil over the pasta and toss to prevent sticking (optional). 5-Heat the pasta sauce in a separate saucepan over low heat. 6-Add the cooked pasta to the sauce and toss until the pasta is coated evenly. 7-Serve hot with grated parmesan cheese or other desired toppings.
